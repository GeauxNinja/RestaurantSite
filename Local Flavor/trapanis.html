One of the Best Places to Eat in The Bay

While our sign says we're the best in Bay St. Louis, many of our fans believe we serve up some of the finest cuisine on the entire Gulf Coast! And we've been doing it for two decades.

A lot has changed since we opened in 1994 - in fact, our home is now a fantastic new two-story building on the beachfront, with a balcony bar upstairs, The Blue Marlin. We can host your holiday gatherings, parties or business meetings too! Just call to reserve a private room or the Blue Marlin.

But we'll never change our commitment to quality, our tip-top service and the family recipes that have made us a regional dining destination.
